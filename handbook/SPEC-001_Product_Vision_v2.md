# SPEC-001 — Product Vision & System Scope

# NexusQuant
## Institutional Quantitative Research & Execution Platform for the Indian Equity Market

**Version:** 2.0 (Vision Locked)

## Executive Summary

NexusQuant is **not** an algorithmic trading bot.

It is an institutional-grade Quantitative Research & Execution Platform where trading is the final outcome of a governed research process.

## Vision

Build the most complete open-source institutional quantitative research platform for Indian equity markets.

## Core Philosophy

- Research is the product.
- Trading is a downstream capability.
- Every production trade originates from validated research.
- Every decision is explainable.
- Every artifact is versioned.
- Every action is auditable.

## Eight Platform Layers

1. Market Intelligence
   - Live feeds
   - Corporate actions
   - Trading calendars
   - Market regime detection
   - Data quality scoring

2. Research Operating System
   - Research projects
   - Hypotheses
   - Experiments
   - Dataset lineage
   - Feature lineage

3. Alpha Factory
   - Indicators
   - Feature engineering
   - ML pipelines
   - Alpha generation

4. Decision Intelligence
   - Confidence scoring
   - Ensemble voting
   - Strategy ranking

5. Portfolio & Risk Intelligence
   - Portfolio construction
   - Exposure
   - Position sizing
   - Risk controls

6. Execution Intelligence
   - OMS
   - Broker abstraction
   - Execution analytics
   - Paper trading

7. AI Quant Copilot
   - Research assistant
   - Strategy review
   - Explainability
   - Report generation

8. Governance & Observability
   - Audit
   - Versioning
   - Monitoring
   - Logging
   - Strategy promotion

## Research Lifecycle

Idea
→ Research Project
→ Dataset
→ Feature Engineering
→ Experiment
→ Validation
→ Backtesting
→ Walk Forward
→ Monte Carlo
→ Paper Trading
→ Production
→ Monitoring
→ Retirement

## Success Criteria

- Real-time market intelligence
- Research governance
- Explainable AI
- Statistical validation
- Institutional risk management
- Portfolio optimization
- OMS integration
- Continuous monitoring
- Complete auditability

## Non-Goals

- Guaranteed profits
- Black-box AI decisions
- Trading without risk approval
- Paid infrastructure dependency during development

## Definition of Done

Every module requires:
- Documentation
- Tests
- APIs
- Versioning
- Observability
- Security review
- Acceptance criteria
